PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

Free Solar Systems for SC/ST/BC Families in AP

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. PM Surya Ghar

Category: Policy Analysis • Updated: February 2026 • Reading Time: 8 minutes

PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana provides free rooftop solar systems to eligible households, including farm-houses. In Andhra Pradesh, the focus is on providing free 2kW solar systems to SC/ST and BC households, targeting nearly 30 lakh families by March 2026.

Scheme Overview

PM Surya Ghar aims to solarize 1 crore households across India, providing free electricity through rooftop solar. Key features:

  • Free Solar Panels: 2kW rooftop system installed at no cost
  • Free Electricity: Generate ~300 units per month
  • Net Metering: Sell excess power to grid
  • 25-Year Life: Long-term free power generation

Who is Eligible in AP?

Priority Categories

  • SC (Scheduled Caste) Households: Full subsidy/free installation
  • ST (Scheduled Tribe) Households: Full subsidy/free installation
  • BC (Backward Classes) Households: High subsidy
  • BPL Families: Below Poverty Line households

General Requirements

  • Own a house/dwelling with suitable roof space
  • Have a valid electricity connection
  • Roof should be suitable for solar panel installation
  • No structural issues with the building

Benefits

Long-Term Value

  • 25 Years: Expected panel life of 25+ years
  • ₹5-8 Lakhs: Total savings over system life
  • Property Value: Increases home value
  • Grid Independence: Reliable power during outages

How to Apply

Step 1: Check Eligibility

  • Confirm your caste/category status
  • Verify roof suitability (shadow-free area)
  • Have electricity consumer number ready

Step 2: Register Online

  • Visit PM Surya Ghar portal (pmsuryaghar.gov.in)
  • Or apply through Meeseva/CSC center
  • Enter consumer number and personal details
  • Upload required documents

Step 3: Feasibility Check

  • DISCOM team will visit for site survey
  • Roof structure and space assessment
  • Feasibility report generation

Step 4: Installation

  • Empanelled vendor assigned
  • Solar panels and inverter installed
  • Net meter connection setup
  • System commissioning

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card
  • Electricity bill/consumer number
  • Caste certificate (SC/ST/BC)
  • BPL card (if applicable)
  • Bank account details
  • Property documents/address proof
  • Passport photographs

For Farm Households

Farmers can benefit from PM Surya Ghar for their residential premises:

  • Reduces household electricity expenses
  • Powers farm equipment during daytime
  • Can be combined with PM-KUSUM for agricultural pumps
  • Complete energy independence possible

For agricultural pump solarization, see our PM-KUSUM Solar Pump Guide.

Current Status in AP

  • Rollout: Currently ongoing
  • Target: 30 lakh families by March 2026
  • Priority: SC/ST households being covered first
  • Progress: Several lakhs already installed
