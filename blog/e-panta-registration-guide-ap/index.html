Register now to qualify for crop insurance and subsidies!

e-Panta (ePanta) is Andhra Pradesh's digital crop booking system that allows farmers to register their crops online. This registration is now mandatory to qualify for government insurance schemes, subsidies, and other agricultural benefits.

Why is e-Panta Registration Important?

Without e-Panta registration, you will NOT be eligible for:

  • Free Crop Insurance under state and central schemes
  • Weather-based crop insurance (WBCIS)
  • Crop loss compensation during natural calamities
  • Input subsidies for seeds, fertilizers
  • MSP procurement benefits

Step-by-Step e-Panta Registration Process

Method 1: Using e-Panta Mobile App (Recommended)

Step 1: Download the App

  • Open Google Play Store on your Android phone
  • Search for "e-Panta" or "AP e-Panta"
  • Download the official app by AP Government
  • Install and open the app

Step 2: Login/Register

  • Select your preferred language (Telugu/English)
  • Enter your registered mobile number
  • Receive and enter OTP for verification
  • Enter your Aadhaar number for authentication

Step 3: Select Land Details

  • Choose your District ‚Üí Mandal ‚Üí Village
  • Your land records will appear automatically (linked via Aadhaar)
  • Select the survey numbers where you're cultivating
  • Confirm the extent (area) of cultivation

Step 4: Enter Crop Details

  • Select the season (Kharif/Rabi/Summer)
  • Choose the crop(s) you are cultivating
  • Enter the sown area for each crop
  • Specify irrigated or rainfed cultivation
  • Add expected sowing/harvest dates

Step 5: Submit and Confirm

  • Review all entered information
  • Submit the registration
  • Note down the registration number/acknowledgment
  • Take a screenshot for your records

Method 2: Through Raithu Bharosa Kendra (RBK)

If you're not comfortable with the app, visit your nearest RBK:

  1. Carry your Aadhaar card and mobile phone
  2. Inform the Village Agriculture Assistant about your crops
  3. They will complete the registration on your behalf
  4. Verify the details shown on screen
  5. Collect the acknowledgment receipt

Method 3: Through Village Revenue Assistant (VRA)

VRAs also assist with e-Panta registration during field visits.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card (linked to mobile number)
  • Mobile Phone (for OTP verification)
  • Land Details (survey numbers - auto-fetched from WebLand)
  • Crop Information (what you're growing)

Crops Covered Under e-Panta

e-Panta covers all major crops grown in Andhra Pradesh:

  • Cereals: Paddy, Maize, Jowar, Bajra, Ragi
  • Pulses: Redgram, Blackgram, Greengram, Bengal gram
  • Oilseeds: Groundnut, Sunflower, Sesame, Castor
  • Cash Crops: Cotton, Sugarcane, Tobacco
  • Vegetables: Tomato, Onion, Chilli, Brinjal, etc.
  • Fruits: Mango, Banana, Papaya, Citrus
  • Spices: Turmeric, Chillies

Benefits of e-Panta Registration

1. Free Crop Insurance

Once registered, you're automatically enrolled for the state's free crop insurance scheme. The government pays the premium on your behalf.

2. Accurate Loss Assessment

During natural calamities, your registered crop data helps in quick and accurate damage assessment and compensation.

3. Better Planning

Government uses aggregated e-Panta data to plan procurement, storage, and market interventions.

4. Digital Record

Your cultivation history is digitally recorded, useful for loan applications and other purposes.

How to Check e-Panta Status

  1. Open the e-Panta app
  2. Login with your mobile number
  3. Go to "My Registrations" or "Status"
  4. View all your registered crops and their status

Common Issues & Solutions

Issue: Land not showing in app

Solution: Your land records may not be updated in WebLand. Visit your Tahsildar office or RBK to update records.

Issue: OTP not received

Solution: Ensure your mobile number is linked to Aadhaar. Update at nearest Aadhaar center if needed.

Issue: App showing error

Solution: Update the app to the latest version. If problem persists, complete registration at RBK.

Issue: Wrong crop registered

Solution: Visit RBK within the registration window to modify your entry.
