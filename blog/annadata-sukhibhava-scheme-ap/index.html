What is Annadata Sukhibhava?

Annadata Sukhibhava (meaning "Happiness of the Food Provider") is the AP government's enhanced farmer welfare program that builds upon and integrates with PM-KISAN. The scheme recognizes that farmers need upfront investment support before each cropping season to purchase seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs.

Key Features:

  • Combined support of ‚Çπ20,000 per farming family per year
  • State contribution: ‚Çπ14,000 (Annadata Sukhibhava)
  • Central contribution: ‚Çπ6,000 (PM-KISAN)
  • Direct Bank Transfer (DBT) to farmer's account
  • Paid in three installments aligned with cropping seasons

Eligibility Criteria

To receive benefits under Annadata Sukhibhava, farmers must meet the following criteria:

Who is Eligible?

  • Land Ownership: Must own agricultural land in Andhra Pradesh
  • Active Cultivation: Land should be under agricultural use
  • Bank Account: Aadhaar-linked bank account mandatory
  • e-KYC: Biometric verification must be completed
  • e-Panta Registration: Crop booking on e-Panta app required for full benefits

Who is NOT Eligible?

  • Government employees (current or retired with pension ‚Çπ10,000+)
  • Income tax payers
  • Professionals (doctors, lawyers, engineers, CAs, architects)
  • Elected representatives (MPs, MLAs, Ministers, Sarpanches)
  • Institutional landholders

How to Register / Check Status

For New Registration

  1. Visit your nearest Raithu Bharosa Kendra (RBK) with:
    • Aadhaar card
    • Land documents (Pattadar passbook/1-B)
    • Bank passbook (Aadhaar-linked account)
    • Mobile number
  2. Complete e-KYC through biometric verification
  3. Register on e-Panta app for crop booking
  4. Verify details and obtain acknowledgment

To Check Payment Status

  • Online: Visit AP Annadata Sukhibhava portal
  • PM-KISAN Status: Check at pmkisan.gov.in
  • Bank: Check your linked bank account
  • RBK: Visit Raithu Bharosa Kendra for assistance

e-KYC: Why It's Critical

The government has made e-KYC (electronic Know Your Customer) mandatory for all beneficiaries. Without completed e-KYC, you may be marked as "ineligible" and miss payments.

How to Complete e-KYC

  1. Visit your nearest RBK or Common Service Center (CSC)
  2. Carry your Aadhaar card
  3. Complete biometric verification (fingerprint/iris scan)
  4. Obtain confirmation receipt

If you haven't completed e-KYC, do it immediately. Farmers without valid e-KYC will not receive their installments. The process takes only 5-10 minutes at any RBK.

e-Panta Registration

The e-Panta (Digital Crop Booking) system is now mandatory for farmers to qualify for insurance and subsidies. All farmers must register their crops on the e-Panta app.

Grievance Redressal

If you haven't received your installment or face any issues:

Grievance Window

The Grievance Module at your local Raithu Seva Kendra (RBK) typically remains open for 7 days after any installment release. Report issues immediately during this window.

Steps to File Grievance

  1. Visit your local RBK within 7 days of installment release
  2. Explain the issue to the Village Agriculture Assistant
  3. Submit written complaint with supporting documents
  4. Obtain grievance ticket number
  5. Track resolution through the grievance portal

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Annadata Sukhibhava different from PM-KISAN?

A: Yes. Annadata Sukhibhava is the AP state scheme providing ‚Çπ14,000/year, while PM-KISAN is the central scheme providing ‚Çπ6,000/year. Together they provide ‚Çπ20,000 annually.

Q: Can tenant farmers receive benefits?

A: The scheme primarily targets landowners. However, AP has provisions for tenant farmer support through other programs. Check with your RBK for specific tenant farmer schemes.

Q: What if I missed the e-KYC deadline?

A: Complete e-KYC immediately. You may receive pending installments once verification is complete, though there might be delays.

Q: How do I update my bank account details?

A: Visit your RBK with new bank passbook (Aadhaar-linked) and request account update. Changes typically reflect within 7-15 days.
