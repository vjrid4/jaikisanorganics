Step 0: Pre-requisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

  • Google Play Developer Account ($25 one-time fee)
  • Jaikisan.aab file (Android App Bundle)
  • signing.keystore file (keep safe!)
  • App icon (512x512 PNG)
  • Feature graphic (1024x500 PNG)
  • Screenshots (minimum 2, recommended 8)
  • Privacy Policy URL
âš ï¸ Important: Keep your signing.keystore file safe! You'll need it for all future app updates. If lost, you cannot update your app.

Step 1: Creating Developer Account

If you don't have a Google Play Developer account:

  1. Go to: play.google.com/console/signup
  2. Sign in with Google Account
  3. Accept Developer Agreement
  4. Pay $25 registration fee
  5. Complete account details:
    • Developer Name: Jaikisan Organics Agro Services
    • Email: contact@jaikisanorganics.com
    • Phone: Your business phone
    • Website: https://jaikisan.co.in

Step 2: Creating the App Listing

  1. Go to: play.google.com/console
  2. Click "Create app"
  3. Fill in:
    • App name: Jaikisan - Farm Fresh Marketplace
    • Default language: English (India) - en-IN
    • App or game: App
    • Free or paid: Free
  4. Accept declarations and click "Create app"
